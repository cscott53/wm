Since 2021, Wafflemania has not only provided enticing breakfasts for on-the-go, drive-through, and dine-in, but we also provide brunch for special events like birthdays, Christmas, and other holidays.

Most of our locations are in Charlotte, including our main location (shown in bottom of page), but we also have a few in Matthews, Pineville, and Gastonia.

Meals are prepared on the spot, and served by our crew members at your service. We have an experienced cooking and catering group that can prepare almost any meal, and we also serve fountain drinks including Kool-aid and smoothies, with a wide variety of flavors.

We also provide plates, silverware, and other items when needed, in case you want a spoon for your eggs, or a straw for your drink.
